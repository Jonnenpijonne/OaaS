# Example — Small Platform Operations

## Purpose

This synthetic example shows how a small platform environment could be represented in the OaaS model.

This is not a real customer environment.

---

## Service overview

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Service name | Example small platform operations |
| Purpose | Keep a small technical platform operable, reviewable and transferable |
| Criticality | Medium |
| Service owner | Service owner role |
| Technical owner | Technical owner role |
| Operations owner | Operations owner role |

---

## Included scope

- runbook maintenance
- incident coordination
- change governance
- service review
- recovery test evidence
- operational handover checklist

---

## Excluded scope

- real customer-specific commitments
- production contracts
- real environment records
- organization-specific policy decisions

---

## Operating cadence

| Activity | Cadence |
| --- | --- |
| Service review | Monthly |
| Recovery review | Quarterly |
| Runbook review | Monthly or after incident |
| Change review | Before planned changes |
| Handover review | Before ownership transfer |

---

## Value

This model reduces operational ambiguity by making ownership, scope, evidence and recovery thinking explicit.
